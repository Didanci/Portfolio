ABOUT ME

I’m Dídac Pardo Martínez, an FX artist specialising in Houdini simulations, motion graphics and video editing. I create cinematic VFX, stylised simulations and environment work for short films and freelance projects.

My workflow spans look development, procedural modelling, pyro/flip/smoke simulations and compositing. I love combining storytelling with technical problem solving.

Available for freelance & contract work. Based in Spain.
